Fixture pools are used to store a universe\textquotesingle{}s patch information. A patch is a pool of addressed fixtures which channel channel values must me comprised in within a 0-\/512 address value interval. A patched fixture\textquotesingle{}s channels may not overlap over the channel addresses of another fixture member of the patch.
