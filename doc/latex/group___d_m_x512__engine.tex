\hypertarget{group___d_m_x512__engine}{}\doxysection{D\+M\+X512 Engine}
\label{group___d_m_x512__engine}\index{DMX512 Engine@{DMX512 Engine}}


The D\+M\+X512 engine is in charge of managing the whole D\+MX show creation and show playback process. To do so, it wraps the functionalities described throughout the apps\textquotesingle{} submodules into higher level functions and data structures to provide users with an easy and intuitive way to create and manage D\+M\+X512 show instances.

\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The D\+M\+X512 engine is in charge of managing the whole D\+MX show creation and show playback process. To do so, it wraps the functionalities described throughout the apps\textquotesingle{} submodules into higher level functions and data structures to provide users with an easy and intuitive way to create and manage D\+M\+X512 show instances.
